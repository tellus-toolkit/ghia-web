Welcome to the GHIA Explore Tool

This tool is currently under development. It will allow you to explore health and greenspace associations in different parts of Greater Manchester.

More information about the project and our other tools are also available.

Project details: 1st August 2016 - 31st July 2019 NERC grant reference number NE/N013530/1
Funders: Natural Environment Research Council, Arts and Humanities Research Council and the Economic and Social Research Council under the Valuing Nature Programme.
