GHIA mapping tools

The GHIA mapping tools provide information about your use of green space in Greater Manchester.

Learn more about GHIA »

Map Query

Use the map to get a report of the type and amount of green cover in your area. You can click a single point, draw your own polygon or select an 'LSOA' census polygon.

A report will be generated with details about the green cover within either a square of 1 km size, inside your drawn polygon or inside the LSOA polygon.

mini Q survey

This survey is for residents of Greater Manchester. The survey asks your views on how urban green spaces and water in Greater Manchester affect your health and wellbeing.

The survey takes about 5-10 minutes to complete and you will then see your results mapped and get an opportunity to see how your views compares with those of other people

Taking part and all questions within this survey are voluntary. From section 2 onwards there are no right or wrong answers - we are interested purely in your opinion.

The information provided by people taking part in this survey will only be used for research purposes. It will not be used for commercial reasons. All data will be treated in accordance with the Data Protection Act (1998).

This is in order to locate your responses on a map anonymously. In circumstances where there are very few households within a postcode area, providing your postcode could link your answers to your household. However, in practice it is unlikely you would be identifiable from providing your postcode, and this is not an aim of the research. Your postcode will not be published, and at no time will your data be used in a way which compromises your right to confidentiality.

Your data will be stored on a secure, password protected server and protected through encryption where appropriate. Anonymised research data will be archived at the UK Data Archive Service or similar, in order to make it available to other researchers in line with current data sharing practices.

It is important to understand that, by saying you consent to taking part in this survey, you agree that:

  • you understand you can withdraw your answers at any time during or at the end of the survey
  • your information will stored in line with data protection policy
  • your words (but no identifiable information) may be quoted in publications, reports, web pages, and other research outputs
  • you consent to confidential and protected data sharing with all members of our research team
  • your information can be archived at a suitable social science data archiving service
  • you understand other genuine researchers will have access to this data and may use your words in research outputs if they agree to preserve your confidentiality as requested in this form

Thank you and we hope you enjoy participating.
